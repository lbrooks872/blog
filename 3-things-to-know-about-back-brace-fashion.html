3 Things to Know About Back Brace Fashion
Written by: Lauren Brooks

Posted on August 1, 2021

1. Braces eat your clothes.

The braces rub against the fabric of clothing and cause white patches that look like chalk marks and can create holes in the fabric. Try covering up screws with Band-Aids or padding if that is what’s causing the problem. Wearing thicker material would be better or wearing a tank top or shirt overtop the brace.

2. Thick and loose clothes are the best option.

Since the braces are big and bulky, eating through clothing like a swarm of moths, the best option of clothing would be something thick and loose. My wardrobe was mainly sweatshirts, flannels, and bulky sweaters. This way, there is a less possibility of holes and the clothes will conceal the brace and undershirt.

3. Hot weather sucks.

When I got my brace, I also got these seamless shirts and seamless tank tops to wear underneath it so the brace wouldn’t rub against my skin. They’re thick, tight, and hot, so it was great in cooler weather but in the 80-degree summer weather I was sweaty and suffering.
