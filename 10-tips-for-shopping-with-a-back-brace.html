10 Tips for Shopping with a Back Brace
Written by: Lauren Brooks

Posted on May 1, 2021

One of the hardest things about wearing a brace is finding the right clothes to conceal the brace while being comfortable and confident. I hope these tips can help you and fit your style!

1. Itâ€™s easier to see the brace through light-colored and thin clothing, so try looking for thicker material and dark colors.

2. Longer shirts hide the brace better.

3. Avoid belts, they press into your skin because of the brace and can cause pain.

4. Leggings are great since there are no buttons or edges that press into skin

5. Scarves can hide ridges from the brace through your shirt.

6. Cardigans can cover any bulkiness in the back.

7. Rompers are loose and great in the heat.

8. Accessories can always brighten up a dull outfit.

9. Stay positive when shopping, it can take time to find the right clothes.

10. Bring a family member or friend when shopping to make it less frustrating.
